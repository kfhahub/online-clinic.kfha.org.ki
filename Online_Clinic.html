About

KFHA (Kiribati Family Health Association) introduces the KFHA Online Clinic, making Sexual and Reproductive Health services accessible from anywhere.


Quality Care from the Comfort of Your Home:

At KFHA Online Clinic, we understand that busy schedules, geographical constraints, or other factors may hinder individuals from accessing traditional healthcare services. Our online platform using the vsee app allows you to receive expert SRHR care from the convenience and privacy of your home, office, or any location with an internet connection.

Services

  • Telehealth Consultations: Schedule a virtual appointment with our experienced healthcare professionals. Our doctors and counselors are ready to address your questions, provide medical advice, and offer personalized support related to family planning, sexual health, and reproductive concerns.
  • Family Planning Counseling: Receive comprehensive family planning counseling to explore different contraceptive options and make informed decisions about your reproductive choices.
  • STI/HIV Consultations: Confidentially discuss concerns related to sexually transmitted infections (STIs) and HIV prevention, testing, and treatment.
  • Pregnancy Support: Expectant mothers can access prenatal care advice and postpartum support from our trained professionals.
  • Youth-Friendly Services: Young individuals can also benefit from youth-friendly virtual consultations tailored to their specific SRHR needs and concerns.

Safe, Confidential, and Convenient:

We prioritize your privacy and well-being. KFHA Online Clinic ensures that all consultations are secure and conducted in a confidential manner. Your personal health information is treated with the utmost care and respect.

How to Access

  1. Download VSee App: Download the VSee app for clients on your smartphone, tablet, or computer. It is compatible with both Android and iOS devices.
  2. Enter the code for your provider: Click here for clinicians code
  3. Input your information: Provide the information and reason for your visit.
  4. Enter waiting room: Then wait for the clinical nurse to respond to you. OR
  5. Schedule an Appointment: Click here to Book an appointment
  6. Join the Consultation: At the scheduled time, join the virtual consultation with our healthcare professional through the app.
